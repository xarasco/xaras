As a follower of Jesus, I believe speaking truth in - and from a place of - love is the right way to treat people.  Of course not all bits of the truth are appropriate to share all the time.  But, I cannot really love if I am not truthful.

This book may be the closest I've seen a business communication book come to this principal.
