# Designing a Game
whoa dang.  It's hard.

## Mechanics
is it a dice builder? deck builder? economic engine? all of those ane more (looking at you TI)

## Rules
how is creativity constrained or encouraged?  

## Story
german/euro-style games have a theme, but what if you want to play out a story or play within a specific universe or system?

## Variability
is it the same thing over and over again or can you try to play a 100 different ways?

## Tech
what if there's something mechanical?  what if there's a remote server and wifi and devices connecting and more...
